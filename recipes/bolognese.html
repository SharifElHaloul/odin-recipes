Spaghetti Bolognese

A simple tomato based Italian Pasta

A simple Bolognese recipe for excellent spaghetti pasta sauce with fresh ingrediants and tons of flavor.

  • 1/2 cup finely diced carrot
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg

  • 1 1.2 cups semi-skimmed milk

  • 1 (28 ounce) can whole italian plum tomatoes (preferably San Marzano)
  • 2 cups water, or as needed
  • Handful of dry spaghetti
  • Boiling salted water for spaghetti
